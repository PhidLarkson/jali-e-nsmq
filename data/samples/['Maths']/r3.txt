The product of two negative numbers is always positive. 
The sum of the interior angles of a triangle is always 180 degrees. 
The square root of a negative number is always imaginary. 
The graph of a linear equation is always a straight line. 
The probability of an event that is certain to happen is always 1. 
The slope of a horizontal line is always 0.
